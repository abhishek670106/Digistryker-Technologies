We Are Increasing Business Success With Technology

Nalia Global Technologies LTD is a company to introduce latest innovative voice and advanced communication services to people in worldwide and provides various types of IT services. Nalia Global Technologies LTD was established in Greece on 2012 and later moved to United Kingdom. We have wide experience in international call termination for CLI Routes, Non-CLI Routes, Call Center Routes, TDM Routes and SMS Termination. Also we  have another department for software development and graphic design as well . We don’t specialize in particular technologies. Every product is unique and we try to fit the right team with the right skills for that particular product.
